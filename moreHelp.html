How exactly does this work?

By dragging blocks to the gray box, you are telling Kristie what to do. She will read through your instructions and do them in order.

Blocks like "attack" tell Kristie exactly what to do once.

Blocks with a little tail are special. The "repeat" block will cause everything inside it to happen again and again.
